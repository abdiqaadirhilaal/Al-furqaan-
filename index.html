Welcome to

Al-Furqaan Quran School

Welcome to Al-Furqaan Quran School, a premier institution dedicated to the memorization and study of the Holy Quran. Our mission is to nurture a generation that not only memorizes the Quran but lives by its teachings and embodies the beautiful character of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).

With qualified teachers, a comprehensive curriculum, and a supportive learning environment, we provide students with the tools they need to excel in their Quranic studies while developing strong Islamic character and values.

Our Programs

Quran Programs

Comprehensive Quranic education programs designed for students of all ages and levels.

Quran Memorization

Structured Hifdh program with daily revisions, new lessons, and regular assessments to ensure strong memorization.

Tajweed Rules

Master the art of Quranic recitation with proper Tajweed rules taught by certified Qaris and qualified instructors.

Islamic Studies

Comprehensive Islamic education covering Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah, Hadith, and Islamic character development.

Arabic Language

Learn Arabic language skills including reading, writing, and conversation to better understand the Quran.
